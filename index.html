The Currency of the Third Wave Society.

Have you ever wondered what CBDCs are? You probably do not care, but you don't have to! In this project our objective is to give our audience an understanding of four things:
1. What the Information Age is and how we are moving away from our current Industrial Society.
2. How the introduction of CBDCs eliminate Cash currency elements of anonymity and integrity.
3. The questioning if blockchain is really decentralized.
4. The potential impacts/uses that CBDCs have, such as being programmed for certain purposes
